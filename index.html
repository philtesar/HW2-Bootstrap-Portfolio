About Me

My name is Phil Tesar and I am currently the Marketing Director at Woodside Event Center in Broadview Heights, Ohio. I have spent my entire 15-year professional career in the Communications and Marketing field. I have worked primarily in the sports industry, having served in a variety of roles with multiple professional sports teams, minor league teams and national sporting events.

I am married to Natalie and we have one son, Malakai (Kai). Natalie is a 2nd Grade teacher in Avon Lake, and we reside in Bay Village.
